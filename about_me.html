Nicholas Jefferis
I was born and raised in Dayton, OH. I graduated from Oakwood High School in 2018 with a 4.2 something GPA. I keep a somewhat active lifestyle playing pickup basketball with my friends and running on occasion. In terms of sports, I probably spend more time watching them than playing them. Having lived in Ohio my whole life, I am a huge Cincinnati fan, supporting the Reds and the Bengals as well as the Cleveland Cavaliers. So far this season (2021), I've been to around eight Reds games and I plan on attending at least one Bengals game to cheer on Joe Burrow and his teammates.
I chose to attend the University of Cincinnati because of a few reasons. Before my first official college tour here, a friend and I attended a FC Cincinnati soccer game at Nippert Stadium, right in the middle of campus. On our way in I was in love. I thought all the different buildings and dense campus was so cool. Then, I learned about how all engineering majors participated in the co-op program. I was fascinated by the idea of gaining work experience and making money to help pay for my tuition.
Outside of sports, there's not much I love more than my cats. The long-haired, grey cat is named Bandit. The other's name is Tiger (because of his stripes). My family adopted him after he visited my house about every day for a year. While I don't live with them anymore, my little sister always sends me cute pictures and funny videos of them.
